Our Story

YOU CAN'T
BUY CULTURE

Safo Sports Group was founded by Michael Ofori-Bah Safo Jr., a University of Maryland student who saw a gap no one was filling: the thousands of college athletes at mid-major schools who have real NIL value but zero representation.

We power SAFO AI and our team of certified agents to connect athletes with brands that actually align with who they are. Beyond the technology, what truly sets us apart is culture. Something you can't replicate, buy, or automate.

Growing up around athletes from marginalized communities, we know firsthand the difference proper representation can make. Our motto? "Worry about ball, and we'll handle the rest."

Our Technology

MEET
SAFO AI

⚡ Powered by AI

SAFO AI stands for Student Athlete Forecasting and Optimization. Our proprietary AI model built from the ground up to connect athletes with their best NIL opportunities.

Athletes may complete a detailed profile, and SAFO AI analyzes the data to determine NIL viability, brand alignment, and earning potential using a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) pipeline backed by athlete and brand data stored via Pinecone vector search.

SSG-signed athletes get the full platform, which is a significantly more detailed version, as well as a dedicated management team to close deals on their behalf. The beta version gives every athlete access to match scores and brand leads at no cost. No signing required.
